HackMotherNature

A Collaborative Sustainability Challenge for SSP students everywhere. Over three days, teams tackle real and imaginative scenarios inspired by regions across the globe—fully asynchronous, with live opening and closing sessions.

Join the Challenge Learn More
  • Join diverse teams representing different continents
  • Tackle three urgent scenarios using research, teamwork, and creativity
  • Build collaboration & critical‑thinking skills with comprehensive study guides
  • Compete for recognition

Why this challenge?

We combine modern problem‑solving with ecological thinking. Teams respond to regional prompts spanning climate, energy, food systems, water, and equity—turning curiosity into actionable ideas.

Interdisciplinary by design: open to every background—no prior experience required.
Asynchronous & inclusive: work on your schedule; meet live to kick off and celebrate.

What you will do

Research & Frame

Design & Prototype

Tell the Story

Three‑Day Flow

Day 1 — Kickoff & Research
  • Live opening (recorded)
  • Team formation & region selection
  • Scenario brief + study guide
Day 2 — Design & Draft
  • Async collaboration
  • Mentor check‑ins
  • Prototype + impact rationale
Day 3 — Polish & Celebrate
  • Submit solution
  • Peer review & judging
  • Live closing & awards

FAQs

Who can participate?

Any SSP student—no prior sustainability or engineering experience required.

Do I need to attend live?

Only the opening and closing sessions are live (and recorded). Everything else is asynchronous.

What do we submit?

A short brief or slide deck covering the problem, your approach, evidence, and expected impact—plus optional visuals or a simple prototype.
