HackMotherNature

HackMotherNature is expanding globally. Alongside fully asynchronous challenges, we now host in-person and online sessions through school clubs and regional chapters. Initially starting in Türkiye and expanding into the United States, HackMotherNature strives to expand and make a global impact.

Join the Challenge Learn More
  • Join diverse teams representing different continents
  • Tackle three urgent scenarios using research, teamwork, and creativity
  • Build collaboration & critical-thinking skills with comprehensive study guides
  • Compete for recognition

Why this challenge?

We combine modern problem-solving with ecological thinking to solve global problems. Teams respond to regional prompts spanning climate, energy, food systems, water, and equity.

Interdisciplinary by Design: Open to every background. No prior experience required!
Asynchronous & Inclusive: Work on your schedule. Meet live to kick off and celebrate!

Three-Day Flow (Hybrid-friendly)

Day 1 — Kickoff & Research
  • Live opening (recorded)
  • Team formation & region selection
  • Scenario brief + study guide
  • Optional regional meet-ups for school clubs
Day 2 — Design & Draft
  • Async collaboration
  • Mentor check-ins
  • Prototype + impact rationale
Day 3 — Polish & Celebrate
  • Submit solution
  • Peer review & judging
  • Live closing & awards

FAQs

Who can participate?

Anybody can participate! No prior sustainability or engineering experience required.

Do I need to attend live?

Many events remain async, but chapters may host live or hybrid sessions.

What do we submit?

A short brief or slide deck covering the problem, your approach, evidence, and expected impact—plus optional visuals or a simple prototype.
